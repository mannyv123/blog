For Scala’s `case classes` we get a sensible `==` operator which is different
from Java’s—it delegates to equals rather than comparing values on reference
identity.

Soooo.

```Scala
new Person("Noel", "Welsh") == new Person("Noel", "Welsh")
// res4: Boolean = true
```
